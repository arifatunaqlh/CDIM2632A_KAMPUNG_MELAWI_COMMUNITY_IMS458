The precious nature at Kampung Melawi

BEACH

Kampung melawi have their own hidden gems. One of the most nature is beach.Beach is nature found in Kampung Melawi. Due to its coastal location, 70% of Kampung Melawi is covered in beaches. The sea provides the majority of the community's income. One of the peaceful and therapeutic settings for people is nature, such as the beach. With the gentle wind and sound of the waves bringing calm to the spirit. The beach has its own distinct character and is surrounded by stunning scenery. Clams, starfish, crabs, and sea snails are just a few of the many beach and marine life that we can locate there. Additionally, the beach is an unique location that is ideal for evening recreation with loved ones. The beach is a beloved location for many people since it offers the ideal environment for relaxing and building relationships with loved ones. However, we are only able to witness the early sunrise from the beach to the east. It's worth getting up early to witness the stunning sunrise views at the eastern beach in the morning. It's a pleasant and enchanting way to begin the day because of the calm atmosphere and breathtaking colours in the sky.

Melawi Beach, D'Aur Beach, Perkasa Beach, and Kuala Rekang Beach are a few of the well-known beaches in the Kampung Melawi area. These beaches captivate tourists because of their individuality and beautiful scenery. The beach is family-friendly and safe for swimming. In addition to having picnics in the evening, both adults and kids enjoy beach bathing. This is due to the beach's high level of safety, which makes it ideal for swimming and other water sports. It's safe when the waves are minor. The beaches in the Kampung Melawi district are also well-known for having soft sand beaches and crystal-clear waters, which make them perfect for swimming and sunbathing. In these charming locations, guests can participate in a variety of water sports like surfing, swimming, and snorkelling.Overall, Kampung Melawi's beaches provide the ideal balance of adventure and leisure for guests of all ages. These beaches offer activities for all types of tourists, whether your preference is to swim or just relax on the sand.
