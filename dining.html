Dining at Kampung Melawi

Local Dining

Celop Tepung Station

Celop Tepung Station is one of the unique dining spots in Kampung Melawi that visitors should not miss. On the edge of Melawi Beach is a little store called Celop Tepung Station, which is owned and operated by locals. It serves a range of menu items, the highlight of which is seafood prepared by dipping it in flour and frying it in oil. The foods are highly well-known, particularly in seaside tourist areas. In addition, Celop Tepung Station serves a selection of desserts such waffles, ice cream and sweet corn, as well as various side dishes like milkshakes. Tourists and locals alike frequent Celop Tepung Station to savour tasty seafood meals because of its welcoming atmosphere and helpful staff.Additionally,the affordable prices and generous portions make it a great value for those looking to experience authentic Malaysian cuisine.

Kedai Mee Celup Kakzah Tepi Padang

Visitors to Kelantan, especially those who stay in beachside villages, should try mee celup. Melawi village is known to one of the community restaurants, Kakzah Mee Celup. There are many menu items here, mostly soups, that come with gravy. This meal is appropriate for enjoying while having fun by the beach in the evening. The food stall also serves required foods and beverages, such as coconut water and shake, which are visitors to the beach' preferred beverages. The Mee Celup is a distinctive and mouthwatering noodle dish that the locals strongly advise experiencing. For those who want to taste real Kelantanese food, Kakzah Mee Celup's savoury broth and fresh seafood combination is a must-try. The cozy atmosphere of the eatery adds to the overall dining experience, making it a memorable meal by the beach.

Mamalicious Mangga Tabur

At Kampung Melawi, Mamalicious Mango Tabur is a popular local restaurant. This shop has opened multiple locations across the country, with one of them located in Kampung Melawi. Fruit juices and smoothies are this store's signature beverages. Mango and dragon fruit smoothies are the most well-known fruit smoothie combinations.These beverages are delightful and incredibly healthful. This establishment serves delicious and well-known drinks in the region. The store's owner is a young, female businesswoman who is driven by her desire to satisfy customers. Therefore, it is highly recommended that you visit and try this fruit juice at the shop.

Featured Restaurant

River Village Rooftop Restaurant

The most well-known restaurant at Kampung Melawi is the River Village Rooftop Restaurant. The rooftop restaurant in Melawi is well-known for its seafood shellout. This is the major menu that draws attention, particularly from guests who bring family, friends, and colleagues. Furthermore, a diverse range of menu options representing Malaysian cuisines, including Chinese, Malay, and Western, are available. Large quantity meal sets that are appropriate for meals for the entire family are also available. Consequently, one of the well-known venues that may be reserved for exclusive gatherings is this dining establishment.

Fizow Kitchen

Famous restaurant Fizow Kitchen is located in Kampung Melawi. This restaurant serves a range of Malay and Western dishes, and serves as a spot where guests come to eat. This place serves fried cuisine, including soups, fried rice, and fried noodles. Western cuisine consists of several types of spaghetti, steak, and chicken chops. This restaurant is a popular with customers, especially young ones, because of how tasty and abundant fast food is. This is also affordable for everyone. All things considered, Fizow Kitchen has a varied menu to suit a range of tastes. It is a well-liked eating destination in Kampung Melawi for both locals and visitors because of its welcoming atmosphere and friendly service.

Kampung Burger

Burgers are the main item on Kampung Burger's menu, which is popular among many people at Kampung Melawi. There are several tempting burger menu options available at Kampung Burger. Lamb, beef, chicken, and rabbit meat burgers are some of the varieties. This restaurant's burger is well-known due to its extraordinarily juicy and flavorful patties. When people want to eat snacks for dinner at night, burger meals are frequently in demand. In addition, this restaurant offers delivery services and an online ordering platform. Customers will therefore find it simpler to make a purchase and eat joyfully as a result. By just making an order, patrons of Kampung Burger may savour their preferred burgers in the convenience of their own homes. The convenience of delivery service ensures that everyone can satisfy their burger cravings without leaving the house.
