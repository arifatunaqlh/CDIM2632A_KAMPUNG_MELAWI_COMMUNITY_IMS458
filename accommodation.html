D'aur Beach Resort

D'Aur Beach Resort is one among Kampung Melawi's accommodation choices. Because the prices are reasonable and you may make amazing memories, this location is incredibly convenient for both tourists and locals. Because of the lovely decorations, which include swings, trees, cottages, and many more, it's also the greatest spot for relaxing and shoot pictures and videos. The hotel accommodations are very reasonably priced and incredibly comfortable. We have to get up early in the morning to anticipate those moments if we want to see the sunrise. Additionally, this hotel room is situated rather close to the beach.There's also a campsite here. Camping is permitted in the region for visitors with their own gear. The welcoming and polite hotel staff goes above and beyond to ensure that guests have a great stay.Also present is a barbecue port. For a wonderful stay, this hotel provides an excellent mix of cost, comfort, and scenic views.

Villa Danialla

At Kampung Melawi, places to stay include Villa Danialla Beach Resort. The villa is located very close to the beach's sandy east coast. In addition to a complimentary breakfast that is included in the room rate, the resort provides free wifi. Situated in a natural environment, Resort Danialla offers comfy accommodations with air conditioning and a neutral colour scheme. The layout of this resort is wide. A massage, a karaoke bar, ping pong and a playground for kids are among the other amenities. The vast menu of both local and foreign cuisine at the Beach Cafe is complemented by the breathtaking view of the sea. In addition to relaxing by the poolside with a cool drink, guests can engage in a range of sports like diving and snorkelling.The friendly staff at Resort Danialla are always ready to assist with any requests or recommendations for exploring the surrounding area
